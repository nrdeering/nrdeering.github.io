Background

Nathan is a data analyst and a business operations professional who works to solve intricate data problems by using his knowledge of different coding languages, mathematics and other technical skills. Nathan has a passion for problem solving and turning numbers into results. Nathan has worked as a data analyst and as a business operations specialist, providing insightful analytics and creating processes to help sreamline the operations of a small business. Currently, he is a Supplier Support Specialist at Lowe's Companies, Inc. and he creates and approves subscriptions for vendors who are looking to sell their products on Lowes.com. Also, he is tasked with automating several of the supplier support team's processes with the use of Python programs and Excel that eliminates tedious tasks. Nathan is a certified data analyst and is LinkedIn certified in Micorsoft Excel and is proficient in Python, SQL, HTML, CSS and a host of other programs and languages. Nathan holds a B.A. in Mathematics for Business from the University of North Carolina at Charlotte and a certification in Data Analytics and Visualizations, also from UNC Charlotte.
